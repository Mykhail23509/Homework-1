sharks to finally play home opener

The San Jose Sharks will play their first home game of the season Saturday after five weeks on the road because of COVID-19 restrictions. The Sharks return to SAP Center against the Vegas Golden Knights and former San Jose coach Peter DeBoer (4 p.m. ET; NBCSCA, ATTSN-RM, NHL.TV). San Jose (5-6-1) played its first 12 games on the road because of restrictions in Santa Clara County, California, that included a ban on contact sports.
